About Me

I am a PostDoc at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT) in the Pattern Analysis and Computer Vision (PAVIS) group. My main research interests lie in Graph Neural Networks, Geometric Deep Learning, and Diffusion Models, with applications spanning Computer Vision, Computational Chemistry, and Natural Language Processing.
