About Me

I am a Research Scientist at Huawei Finland R&D and a Visiting Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Cambridge in the group of Prof. Pietro Liò[cite: 21]. My work focuses on developing state-of-the-art generative models [cite: 4] and Geometric Deep Learning frameworks[cite: 4].

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Milano-Bicocca (2023)[cite: 53, 56]. I have been honored with the Outstanding Individual Contribution Award at Huawei [cite: 12] and the ELSA mobility grant[cite: 20]. My expertise spans computer vision, autonomous driving, and computational chemistry[cite: 5].
