About Me

I am Stefano, a Research Scientist at Huawei Finland R&D and a visiting researcher at the University of Cambridge (group of Prof. Pietro Liò). Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT) in the PAVIS group supervised by Alessio Del Bue, Ph.D.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Milano-Bicocca in 2023. My work focuses on pushing the boundaries of Generative Models, Graph Neural Networks, and Autonomous Driving.
